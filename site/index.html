Good to know

Quick answers for first-time visitors heading to Main Street.

Where is Whistle Stop located?

Whistle Stop Grill & Bar is located at 915 Main Street in downtown Safety Harbor, Florida 34695.

What are the restaurant hours?

We are open Sunday through Thursday from 11 AM to 9 PM, and Friday through Saturday from 11 AM to 10 PM.

Is the patio dog-friendly?

Yes. Our open-air Main Street patio is dog-friendly and built for relaxed Safety Harbor hangs.

Do you host live music and events?

Yes. Check the events calendar for live music, open mic nights, cornhole, book club, happy hour, and community nights.

Where can I view the menu?

View the main menu, seasonal menu, and bar menu on our menu page before you visit or order online.
