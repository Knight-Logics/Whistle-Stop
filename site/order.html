Grubhub vs. "their own program"

Grubhub.com is Grubhub's platform — restaurants create a merchant listing; customers order through Grubhub's app/site. Whistle Stop does not host a white-label ordering site on Grubhub unless they purchased Grubhub Direct (custom URL). Typical setup: pickup + delivery only. Catering is a separate Grubhub product — only show it if they actually enabled group catering in their merchant portal.
